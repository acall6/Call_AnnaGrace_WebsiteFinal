Located in Chicago, Kasama is the first Michelin Starred Filipino restaurant. It is owned and run by chef couple Tim Flores and Genie Kwon, who have a passion for bringing Filipino food to their customers. They serve breakfast, lunch, dinner, and dessert, in a fine-dining atmosphere with luxurious quality food.
